Get Involved

Adopt a Dolphin is a volunteer-led organisation. The organisation has no paid officers and uses all donations towards animal and environmental programs: direct rescue, educational programs and animal sanctuaries. Utilising volunteerism, Adopt A Dolphin has kept operating costs to a bare minimum while maximising our ability to help animals.

Facts

Dolphins are often regarded as one of earth's most intelligent animals. They communicate like humans and can establish strong social bonds; they will stay with injured or ill individuals, even helping them to breathe by bringing them to the surface if needed.

Dangers

Various fishing methods, most notably purse seine fishing for tuna and the use of drift and gill nets, kill many dolphins. By-catch in gill nets and incidental captures are common and pose a risk for mainly local dolphin populations.
